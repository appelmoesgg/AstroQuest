Welcome, Space Explorer!

Your mission is to explore the cosmos and gather valuable resources.

Click the "Gather Resources" button to manually collect resources.

Unlock the "Asteroid Miner" to automatically gather resources for you.

Upgrade your spaceship to increase your resource gathering efficiency.

Discover new planets and reach for the stars!


Prestige Mechanic:

After exploring the universe and amassing experience, you can choose to prestige. Prestiging resets your progress but grants you a permanent bonus, making future explorations more fruitful.

Collect prestige points by discovering planets. Every time you prestige, you gain 1 additional maximum asteroid miner, allowing you to gather resources more efficiently in future journeys.
